About Me

As a web developer, I envision myself working to innovate and improve the lives of everyone. I earned a math degree from Mount Saint Mary College, so my whole life I’ve been driven towards analytical pursuits and activities that promote intellectual growth. My main areas of interest, along with coding, are statistics and economics.

I’m a numbers guy who can and has dealt with many complex algorithms to arrive at a right answer. While coding isn’t exactly the same way, I have a passion for analysis. My main strength is I can rapidly pick up new skills or deal with new information. How my passion for analysis relates to my interest in tech is that I see it as a new language with a huge impact on the future infrastructure of society, and I would do well to serve that. In this rapidly growing field, I understand that I need to constantly improve my skills so I can provide top notch service.
